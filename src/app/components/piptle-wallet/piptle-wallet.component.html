Withdrawal Conditions and Timeframes Notice:

It is important to understand that you please input the correct wallet address for sending your coins or tokenstoo.

Please ensure you have correctly checked all characters and the amount you are wishing to send prior tosending. There should be no spaces after the last character in your wallet address as this will impede theability to finalise your transaction.

In most cases your coins or tokens will be sent immediately after you have approved the withdrawalconfirmation email.

Although most coin and token withdrawals are processed immediately, withdrawals can take up to 12 hours.We endeavour to process your withdrawals and have them broadcast on the blockchain as soon as possible.However, we are unable to guarantee instant withdrawals.

If you intend to have time-sensitive withdrawals, we strongly advise that you store your coins and tokens ona private wallet.

Most automatically processed coin and token withdrawals from your ILO wallet will be processed within afew minutes, but if manual approval is required or our hot wallet has insufficient funds and requires to berefilled via manual intervention.

In situations where there is maintenance on a wallet, our wallets are syncing or we have an unexpectedoutage, the withdrawal may be longer than 12 hours.

You can check the status of your transactions via the "Transaction" Tab on each wallet page. If the status is"pending", please check you email inbox for any correspondence from us.

If your withdrawal has been pending for an extended period of time, we may be able to cancel yourwithdrawal and return the funds to your wallet. Please contact support for further information.
