Tokenomics & Vesting Details

The seed round allocates 32 million $EMYA tokens, which represents 3.2% of the total supply of 1 billion tokens.

At the TGE, 5% of the seed round allocation will be unlocked, equivalent to 0.16% of the total token supply.

There is a 3-month cliff period, during which no tokens will be unlocked.

After the 3-month cliff period, the remaining tokens will be released daily over 14 months, completing the total vesting period of 17 months (including the cliff).

Link Program

Participants can share their unique Code or URL to invite others to participate in the $EMYA Seed Round. When someone invests using your Code, you earn a Scout fee in USDT based on their purchase amount.

The commission structure is as follows:

  • 5% for your Tier 1
  • 3% for your Tier 2
  • 2% for your Tier 3

All Scouting Fees earned through the LINK program will be sent in USDT directly to your wallet in 24 hours of Token purchase confirmation.

You can find your LINK URL on your LINK Tab. Share this URL with your Community via social media, email, or direct messages to start earning Scout Fees.

Your Scouting fees will be displayed in your LINK tab, along with details of the LINKed participants.
